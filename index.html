Privacy Policy

Bluetooth Brain by Asterisk Computing

Effective Date: November 28, 2025

Last Updated: November 28, 2025

Summary: Bluetooth Brain does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app data stays on your device.

Introduction

Bluetooth Brain ("the App") is developed and maintained by Asterisk Computing ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

This privacy policy explains what information the App accesses, why it needs certain permissions, and how your data is handled.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Bluetooth Brain operates entirely on your device. We do not:

  • Collect personal identifiers
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts or messages
  • Store data on external servers
  • Share any information with third parties

Data Storage

All data created by the App (your device profiles and settings) is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Device profile configurations (Bluetooth device names, MAC addresses, volume settings)
  • App preferences (theme, notification settings)

This data never leaves your device and is automatically removed if you uninstall the App.

Permissions We Request

The App requires certain Android permissions to function. Here's why each permission is needed:

Permission Purpose
Bluetooth Detect when Bluetooth devices connect or disconnect to trigger your profiles
Bluetooth Connect Read the names and addresses of paired Bluetooth devices
Foreground Service Run in the background to monitor Bluetooth connections
Notifications Show a persistent notification while the service is running
Boot Completed Automatically start monitoring after device restart
Modify System Settings Adjust screen brightness when profiles are applied (Premium feature)
Internet Display advertisements (free version) and verify in-app purchases

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob (Free Version Only)

We display banner advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device's Google settings.

Google Play Billing

In-app purchases are processed through Google Play. Purchase information is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. The App does not contain content inappropriate for any age.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and we do not operate any servers, there is no risk of data breach from our side. Your device profile data is stored in your device's private app storage, which is protected by Android's security model.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
